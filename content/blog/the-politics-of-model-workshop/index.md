I am happy to share that I am co-organizing the Workshop **"The Politics of Models - Bias, Representation & Transparency in Generative Models"**, taking place on 25-27 August 2026 at Rånäs Slott, Sweden.

The workshop is part of WASP and WASP-HS and will bring together researchers working on questions related to bias, representation, transparency, accountability, and the broader impact of generative AI.

Researchers from WASP and WASP-HS are welcome to apply. The deadline for expression of interest is 17 April 2026, and the workshop is limited to 20 participants.

I am looking forward to the Conversations and ideas that will come out of it.

### Organizers

- Kivanç Tatar, Chalmers University of Technology
- Kathlén Kohn, KTH Royal Institute of Technology
- Md Fahim Sikder, Linköping University
- Francis Lee, Södertörn University
- Bodil Formark, Södertörn University

More details: https://wasp-hs.org/event3/workshop-the-politics-of-models-bias-representation-transparency-in-generative-models/
