One of the most rewarding parts of working on synthetic data is seeing it spark genuine curiosity beyond the technical community. I am proud to be co-organizing **"Humanities Hackathin - AI Generated Texts and Images"**, which brings humanists and AI researchers into the same room to ask hard questions about how synthetic data can and should be used in historical and literary research. 

Join us in Uppsala on May 25.

Registration closes May 15.

More details: https://ai-history-hackathon.fahimsikder.com/
